About Us

We (founders Luke and Ozzy) started Loz Digital after teaming up on a project involving automation in the aviation industry. Since then, we've realized the great potential in applying data, programming and design thinking to solve unique problems and find ways to improve processess through automation and logical thinking.

Our Process

Our approach is simple. We spend plenty of time understanding and defining the problem and then break it down into as many fundamental component parts as possible. We make sure we clearly understand what each piece does and then rebuild from the ground up to create intuitive, highly functional products. The main components of problem solving stay the same, although the techniques and tools we use will always vary.
