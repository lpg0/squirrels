H.E.L.P.

Homeschool Education & Learning Planner (H.E.L.P.) should make the process of homeschooling more accessible and accommodating for parents, students, and educators alike. H.E.L.P. will allow homeschooling parents to plan a student’s coursework daily, semesterly, and yearly in order to fulfill all the necessary requirements for graduation on their timeline. Parents would also gain access to view student information such as transcripts and progress reports which they can then send to the county and state. Additionally, homeschooled students will be able to view their academic progress through a gradebook as well as access supplementary resources like quizzes and Khan Academy. H.E.L.P. should let parents, children, and educators navigate the website through its clear aesthetics and concise layout. Members of the Board of Education, Department of Education, Homeschool Legal Defense Association, etc. will be able to update and correct information.
